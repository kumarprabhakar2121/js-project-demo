THE PERFECT PAIR

Bar X was opened the year Prohibition was repealed in 1933. After our purchase and refurbishing, in 2010, it became the home of craft cocktails and long Utah nights. After 3 years our little baby started begging for a sibling so we gave her Beer Bar. Bar X and Beer Bar now stand as companion pieces. In the mood for a meticulously prepared cocktail with fresh ingredients? Step through the magic door to Bar X. Did you get hungry and wonder what the score was? Step back through to Beer Bar. Just don’t stand in the doorway, it's not huge.THE PERFECT PAIR.

BEGINNINGS

This little ditty begins in a car. Our two owner/operators, Richard Noel and Duncan Burrell, hopped in a rental car from PDX and began a tour of northwest Oregon. Meeting with brewery owners and brewers along the way, Rich and Dunc promised quality and attention to detail if they'd entrust us with their beers. Luckily they did. That journey continued to other states and will continue to grow as we spread the word that we always pay attention to glassware, temperature and dates for each beer. These aren’t the worst business trips.

PROVISIONS

Like many restaurants across the country, people are learning that they don’t need to leave their area code to put the highest quality of food on their tables. We’re no different. When we decided to create a simple beer garden menu we looked no further than Viet Pham and Frody Volgger. With the help of our executive chef, Brendan Kawakami, we've managed to find some of the best local purveyors Utah has to offer. Including: Salt & Smoke Meats, Eva’s Bakery, Charming Beard Coffee, & Vosen’s Bakery. Also, the fries are really, really good.
